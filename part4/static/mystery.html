CASE CLOSED

Room 4, Hôtel Sauvage

Meiringen, Switzerland — 1891

Owner: Estate of J. Moriarty, disputed

Price: Not disclosed

A small, spare room on the first floor of the Hôtel Sauvage, overlooking the valley. The Reichenbach Falls are visible from the window — a white column of water dropping into nothing. The room was last occupied on the night of 4 May 1891 by a guest registered under the name Sigerson. His luggage remained. His bed was not slept in. The path to the falls was found disturbed the following morning. No body was ever recovered. The hotel has not taken a booking for this room since.

Amenities: A view that changes everything.
